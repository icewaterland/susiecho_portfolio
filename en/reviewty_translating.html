Overview

Brief

The Reviewty translation platform is that translates all cosmetic information in the Reviewty into Vietnamese. A certified translator can select the content which needs to be translated, translate it, and create profit.

The Task

I have to solve the content translation problem required for the Reviewty service. The primary purpose of this solution is to enhance the competitiveness of the service by providing high-quality Vietnamese information on cosmetics that will be exposed to the Reviewty. In addition, it should be possible to execute and manage a large amount of translation with a reasonable budget.

Problem

The Reviewty is a cosmetic commerce platform that provides Vietnamese consumers with information on cosmetics that are currently lacking in the Vietnamese market and connects purchases based on this. As such, we tried to provide accurate information about cosmetics in Vietnamese, but we faced some problems.

  • The vast amount of content for the first launch goal of over 270 million characters in Korean

  • A limited time until the first launching and a fixed schedule for all the makers

  • Insufficient primary translation target budget

  • The goal of providing high-quality translation content beyond machine translation to secure competitiveness

  • The limited workload for translation-related personnel

Goal

  • To convert vast amounts of content in a short time and use it as data for the product

  • To be able to calculate and adjust the budget according to the content supply and demand and to manage the budget rationally

  • To provide high-quality translated content to users

  • To continuously secure and easily manage the translator manpower

  • To allow internal administrators to spend minimal time managing translations

Proposal

The core content of Reviewty is professional and extensive information on cosmetics. I proposed to develop its translation system to provide it in Vietnamese. The Reviewty translation platform translates Korean information into high-quality Vietnamese quickly and enables budget calculation and adjustment according to the supply and demand of translation of content. Internal managers will be able to work efficiently with a minimum of time on management issues related to translation also budget.

In addition to this, it is significant to form our translator manpower to offer newly added information to the Reviewty users stably. Translators can be encouraged to continue translating by getting differential rewards according to the priority or difficulty of the content.

Define

Problem

The problems to be solved in the translation part during the process of launching the Reviewty are as follows :

  • The amount of information targeted for the first launching of Reviewty is vast, with more than 270M in the number of Korean characters and so on.

  • Due to the limited time until the first launching of Reviewty and the schedule of all stakeholders set for it, there is a limit to the resources that can be utilized.

  • Likely, the primary translation budget would not be able to complete the amount of primary target content by utilizing the market's translation platform or hiring a professional translator.

  • To get the competitiveness of a product, high-quality translation, not machine translation, is required.

  • Have to minimize the translation-related workload of the operation and accounting team, which consists of limited manpower.

Options for Solution

Mindmap

Classify & Develop

I judged that self-development of the translation platform is the most rational way to solve the problems we are facing. I defined the core missions of the product and derived ideas corresponding to each item.

How can we get an accurate translation quickly?

  • Translation work page UX design : Provides detailed information such as related photos and categories for a quick understanding of the context

  • Payment : Build trust through payments, provide accurate deposits for date and amount, and provide payment history confirmation

  • Case study : Provides search and browsing function for approved content to refer to words, styles, and similar products

  • Content Level : Increase competition by branching levels and pricing higher according to the length and importance of content

  • Notification : Because it is often updated in bulk, provide email notification when a certain amount of content is updated

How can we use my budget effectively?

  • Unit price adjustment according to supply and demand : Designed to allow manual unit price adjustment according to the amount of content waiting for translation and the number of translators

How can we do a translation approval quickly and accurately?

  • Typo check : Approval page design to enable basic typo inspection

  • The approval status of the translator : Offer the translator's accumulated approval status on the approval page

How can we recruit a large number of talented translators?

  • Referrals and invitations : Provide points which can convert to cash after registration or translation through recommendation and invitation functions

  • Targeted advertising : Promote Korean department of Vietnamese university and Facebook translation-related groups

  • Required the language test certificate attached : Language test certificate must be attached and upon expiration, suspension of activity until renewal
