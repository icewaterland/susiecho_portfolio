Overview

Brief

K-town is a local business search service and marketing platform for Koreans living in Vietnam and the northeastern US. General users can monitor information about Korean businesses in the desired region and promotions. And they can obtain additional information through reviews of other users. Anyone can register their business and can promote through payment.

The Task

Over the past year, users' discontent with the app service has increased, and the service usage rate has decreased. Identify the inconvenience experienced by users and solve it. Efficiently utilize the limited schedule and manpower and complete product improvement without changing the existing main logic as much as possible.

Problem

The mobile K-town service, which was first launched in 2013, The update of core functions to solve the needs of mobile users has been pended due to web-oriented maintenance. Moreover, It has been operated as a response from internal personnel when it has an urgent issue. As a result, operating costs are increased and user inconvenience is continuously accumulated, and the number of mobile users is gradually decreasing.

  • The product that did not take advantage of native apps

  • Uncleared UX design with blurred user roles

  • Low aesthetics and unclear branding, demoting the attractiveness of the product and visual consistency with the web

  • Incorrect design such as confusing UX writing and repetitive functions placement

  • Existing designs are based on the premise that the service is operated by internal personnel

Goal

  • Provide mobile optimization services by actively utilizing the strengths of native app

  • Minimize the work of in-house managers by providing products that users can independently utilize

  • Build a design system that can maintain consistency even with sporadic updates

  • Deliver consistency and aesthetics by clarifying the brand identity

  • Offering the features and satisfying experiences to users need to reach their goals

Proposal

Separate the user roles to satisfy them who have different purposes each. In addition, increase the attractiveness of the service by improving the confusing UX and low-esthetic UI of the existing service. Moreover, build the design system to maintain visual consistency even when the team changes and updates are in progress.

The most significant part of the project is, to precisely define users according to their needs and to remove confusion by refining the functions they need to fulfill their purpose. It will help users to make use of K-town as a habit.

Hypotheses

Through the above process, I discovered the initial needs that the service wanted to satisfy and established the complaints users encountered while using the service as an initial hypothesis. Through this hypothesis, I tried to define the problems that users experience accurately.

User needs

  • General user : Want to check business information before visiting and know if there are any discounts that I can get

  • General user :Want to know if there are any good places, special events or discounts in specific area

User frustrations

  • There are so many search buttons that I don't know how to find the information I'm looking for

  • Want to find information about a new store or decent business nearby, not a specific business.

Problem

Enhanced the weakened mobile user experience due to web-centered sporadic updates and improved the brand experience faded over time. In addition, designed that users to utilize the features independently without relying on internal administrators.

Target & Persona

The main users of the service are parents of international students, the self-employed, office workers, and international students in that order. They range from late teens to mid-50s and are used by a wide range of age groups, but 35-45 years old users are the most frequent. There was no significant difference in the ratio of male and female users, but the number of female users was slightly higher. Based on this, I defined the persona for establishing a product improvement plan.

Design Principles

In the process of checking the problems of existing services, established the main principles of improvement. It is a story about not only the product itself but also can be applied in the process of development and design, and then shared with team members.

Seamless integration

A product with users with different purposes is designed so that users are not confused and minimize friction in the process of reaching each purpose.

Simple and Clear

Design the development results and processes simply and clearly, from huge core elements such as priorities and service hierarchy to delicate constituents efficiently.

Follow the design system

Build design system for easy and clear handovers and consistent brand and experience in a company environment that changes the team frequently.

Scope

The development scope of the project is set as the realization of the K-town function of the portal service web service to the level of mobile optimization. However, even if it affects the structure of the web, discussed so that it can be included in the scope of development if it is of high importance.

I archived low priority items that are not included in this project scope in the requirement definition and handover them to keep going to develop by the next team.

Ideate

Ideate

Mindmap

Freely expand ideas around defined problems. It focuses on writing as many solutions and functions as possible to solve problems without restrictions on development resources and development methods.

Classify & Develop

Select ideas that are useful to solve problems from the mind map and then group them. To develop this in detail, I defined it as follows and discussed whether the final development is possible.

How can users utilize the service more easily?

  • Divide the user role : Divide the roles into two users according to the main purpose of the users who use the service.

  • Usability improvements : Get the most out of native apps' advantages, including camera, library, GPS, support for switching to other helpful apps, and more to help users reach their end goal.

How can users quickly reach accurate information?

  • Improving the bad UX : Offer a search experience that removes users' frustration by improving the search UX, which has increased confusion.

  • Information hierarchy design : Design the detailed information page by rearranging the information, according to the priority so that the user can recognize the information at an appropriate speed. Structure additional information that can be selected according to need.

How can the product be attractive to business users?

  • Store Information CRUD : Change the structure to the business users register and manage the information directly, and the service manager approves it. It helps to reduce the workload of internal personnel and provides the latest information to general users.

  • Manager appointment function : To expand the operation method of business users, divide the business management user into two kinds, admin and manager. The two roles are different depending on whether payment is possible.

  • Booking : Offer benefits to general users when making a reservation, and raise retention by designing business users to manage their reservations. For this, add functions such as reservation function, time change request, and chat.

  • Statistical function : Provide useful statistical data related to their business information to business users. Motivate payment by providing expansion price plans according to their needs.

How can offer a consistent and beautiful product?

  • Improving brand design : IImprove the brand identity to be consistent with the design identity of the web product.

  • Product design system design : Configure the design system to keep the consistency of UX¬∑UI design, in preparation frequently changing the team for the company characteristics.

  • Code-based design : To efficiently respond to app capacity issues and resolution, develop a code-based design rather than the existing image-based design.

Promotion of my business,
How can operate easily and efficiently?

Intuitive UX to reach purpose the user

After a period, the number of internal users increased and got a good evaluation from users and the CEO. However, I could not check in detail.
